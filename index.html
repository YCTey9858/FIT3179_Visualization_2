World Universal Health Coverage Index of Service Coverage

Universal Health Coverage Index is is an index reported on a unitless scale of 0 to 100, which is computed as the geometric mean of 14 tracer indicators of health service coverage. From the map, we can observe that developed country such as United States, Canada, Australia are having a high UHC index above 80 in 2015. This means that these countries are having nice healthcare service and infrastructure for their citizen. Thus, the higher the UHC index, the people in that country will be more healthier.
